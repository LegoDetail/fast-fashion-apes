Fast Fashion
By Sam Sussman-Randall
The Basic Problem
People Buy Newer Clothes To Keep Up With Trends Leading To Clothes Being Used Less And Thrown Out Sooner
Social View
Because The Issue Of Fast Fashion Is Lead Largely By Quick Changing Trends, There Is A Positive Feedback Loop Increasing The Trend Of Quick Trends
Economic View
As New Fashion Trends Become Popular, New Clothes Are Made That Use/Waste Many Limited Resources. The Trend Of Fast Fashion Also Encourages Lower Quality Materials And Products That Wear Out Sooner And End Up Costing More.
Environmental View
More Clothes Are Made That Use Many Resources Such As Cotton, Fossil Fuels, And More. The Quick Harvest Of These Resources Often Create Pollution And/Or GHG. Clothes Are Then Thrown Out And Often End Up In Landfills As Waste.
Solution View
To Help You Should Use Your Clothes For Longer Before Getting New Clothes. To Go Along With That It Helps To Get A Few Higher Quality Clothes That You Can Wear For A Long Time
